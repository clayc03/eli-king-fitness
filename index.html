About Eli King Fitness

The Ultimate Fitness Way to Change Your Life!

Suitable for Beginners

The Beginner program is designed for anyone with none to very little experience. Group lessons will make you feel at ease and get you going from the first class.

Advanced Techniques

The advanced program focus on the fitness enthusiasts who are willing to go that extra mile. Group lessons will get you going from the first minute and focus and determination will get you through a HIIT class.

Personal Guidance

Personal training sessions for Beginner or Advanced start of with a one on one session. I will get to know you and design a fitness program that suites your needs. A Progress report will be done on a weekly basis in a one on one session.

The Facility

As an independant registered and certified personal trainer all my classes are done at a Privately Owned gym. Group classes are done daily, six days a week (Monday - Friday) and all personal training gym sessions. The gym make use of the latest equipment and change room facilities.
